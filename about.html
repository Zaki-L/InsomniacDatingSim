Game

Storyline

In your apartment as the clock ticks past midnight, you notice your roommate, Oliver, struggling with sleeplessness. Concerned for his well-being, you felt the urge to intervene and help him find respite from the torment of insomnia. With your choices, will you make him sleep or not? The outcome rests in your hands, determining whether your roommate finds peaceful slumber or continues to battle with his insomnia.

Tips and Tricks

To win the game, make wise choices in your communication options. Select options that offer techniques or methods to help your roommate sleep. When interacting with your roommate in the game, choose dialogue options that show understanding of his situation.
