INSOMNIAC DATING SIM

How to play


In the opening scene of the game, the player checks up with his/her roommate, who suffers from insomnia. The objective is to help them sleep by selecting options provided throughout the game. Your decisions will lead to either a positive or negative outcome. Achieving a good ending through your chosen options means successfully helping your roommate in sleeping, resulting in victory. Conversely, failing to help your roommate sleep will result in a bad ending, leading to defeat.
